Avoid These Common Mistakes

Panic Selling

Selling when markets drop locks in losses

Instead: Stay invested. Markets recover!

Timing the Market

Waiting for the "perfect" time to invest

Instead: Invest regularly, regardless of market conditions

High Fees

Paying 1-2% in fees can cost you hundreds of thousands

Instead: Choose low-cost index funds (< 0.20% fees)

Not Diversifying

Putting all money in one stock or sector

Instead: Spread across 15-30 holdings or use ETFs

Emotional Investing

Following hype, FOMO, or fear

Instead: Stick to your plan and strategy

Not Starting

Waiting until you have "enough" money

Instead: Start with whatever you can today!
